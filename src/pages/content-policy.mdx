## Our Philosophy

This marketplace exists to support authentic relationships and high-quality exchanges within trusted communities. We welcome items and services that reflect our core values: putting people before profit, prioritizing quality over quantity, and fostering genuine connections.

If you're unsure whether something belongs here, ask yourself: "Does this help build trust and add value to my community?" If the answer is yes, it probably belongs here.

</Card>

## Guiding Principles

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-8 my-12">

<Card>

### Legal and Safe

All items and services must comply with local, state, and federal laws. Items that could pose safety risks should include appropriate warnings and safety information.

</Card>

<Card>

### Honest and Accurate

Descriptions should be truthful and complete. Share both the strengths and limitations of what you're offering. Building trust means being transparent.

</Card>

<Card>

### Community-Focused

We prioritize small-scale, local commerce and authentic human connections. Mass-produced items from major retailers don't fit our mission, but local small businesses absolutely do.

</Card>

<Card>

### Respectful

Items and services should respect the dignity and wellbeing of all people. Content that promotes harm, hatred, or exploitation has no place here.

</Card>

</div>
