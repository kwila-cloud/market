# Content Policy

The following items are acceptable to list on this marketplace:

- Baked goods
- Handmade clothing
- Handmade crafts
- Custom 3D prints
- Used farm and yard equipment
- Used appliances
- Software services
- Home cleaning services
- Baby sitting services
